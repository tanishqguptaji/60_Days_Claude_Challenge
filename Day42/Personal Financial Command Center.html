Financial Literacy
Tips, checklists & resources
Curated for freelancers managing mixed income streams.
01
Separate business & personal accounts
Open a dedicated business checking account so income, taxes, and expenses never blur together.
02
Pay yourself a "salary"
Move a fixed amount to personal checking each month regardless of when client payments land, smoothing irregular income.
03
Set aside taxes on receipt
The moment a payment lands, transfer your set-aside percentage before you can spend it.
04
Build a 4–6 month buffer
Irregular income needs a bigger cushion than a salaried job — aim higher than the standard 3-month rule.
05
Attack high-APR debt first
Credit card APRs compound fast. Extra payments toward the highest-rate balance save the most over time.
06
Automate small, consistent investing
Even $50–150/month into a diversified low-cost fund compounds meaningfully over a decade.
