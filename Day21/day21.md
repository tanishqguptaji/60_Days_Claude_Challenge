# Day 21 – Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective

Today's challenge focused on using Claude to build a modern Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard that analyzes a user's reported digital ecosystem and visualizes privacy exposure, tracking risks, company concentration, and actionable privacy improvements.

The dashboard transforms a simple list of applications into an interactive cybersecurity-style analytics experience.

---

# Challenge Overview

Claude was used as:

- Privacy Intelligence Analyst
- Cybersecurity Dashboard Designer
- Data Visualization Assistant
- Risk Assessment Engine

The objective was to generate a complete self-contained HTML dashboard with interactive visualizations and privacy insights.

# Privacy Improvement Plan

Recommended actions generated by the dashboard:

### 1. Audit App Permissions

Review and revoke:

- Location
- Contacts
- Camera
- Microphone

permissions for unnecessary applications.

---

### 2. Enable Platform Privacy Controls

- Private Instagram account
- Snapchat privacy settings
- Disable personalized advertising

---

### 3. Replace Google Search

Use DuckDuckGo or privacy-focused alternatives to reduce search profiling.

---

### 4. Use Signal for Messaging

Reduce metadata collection by migrating sensitive conversations to Signal.

---

### 5. Review TikTok Permissions

Restrict unnecessary permissions or uninstall if not required.

---

### 6. Separate Email Addresses

Use dedicated emails for:

- Shopping
- Payments
- Social Media

to minimize cross-platform identity linkage.
