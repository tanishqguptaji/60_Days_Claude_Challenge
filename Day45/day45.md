# Day 45 — AI Decision Strategist

## Project Overview

Today I built an AI-powered Decision Strategist that helps users evaluate difficult life and career decisions using structured reasoning instead of emotional reactions.

The application conducts a short interview, analyzes each option objectively, identifies hidden assumptions and cognitive biases, compares alternatives through a weighted decision matrix, and generates an interactive decision report.

Unlike a traditional pros-and-cons list, this project focuses on helping users think clearly and confidently before making important decisions.

---

## Features

### Decision Interview

- Four guided questions
- One question at a time
- Minimal conversation flow
- Personalized decision context

---

### Decision Report

Generates a complete dashboard containing:

- The Real Decision
- Case for Each Option
- Assumption Buster
- Decision Matrix
- Premortem Analysis
- 7-Day Validation Plan
- Final Verdict
- Shareable Summary Cards

---

### Decision Matrix

Evaluates every option across seven dimensions:

- Career Upside
- Financial Safety
- Growth & Learning
- Stress Level
- Reversibility
- Long-Term Alignment
- Regret Risk

Animated score bars make comparison easy.

---

### Assumption Analysis

Highlights:

- Hidden assumptions
- Cognitive biases
- Overlooked factors

Helping users avoid emotional decision-making.

---

### Premortem Planning

Simulates failure after twelve months and identifies:

- Possible reasons
- Early warning signs
- Preventive actions

---

### 7-Day Decision Plan

Provides a structured framework for validating important decisions before committing.

---

### Verdict

Summarizes:

- Recommended option
- Supporting reasoning
- What could change the recommendation
- One difficult truth

---

### Shareable Cards

Compact cards designed for screenshots:

- Matrix Summary
- Final Verdict
- LinkedIn-ready reflection
