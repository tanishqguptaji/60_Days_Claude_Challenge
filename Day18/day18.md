# Day 18 – Brain Dump Action Planner Skill

## Objective

Today's challenge focused on creating a reusable Claude Custom Skill that transforms unstructured notes, meeting transcripts, brainstorming sessions, and voice memos into structured project dashboards and action plans.

The goal was to eliminate manual note organization by automatically generating:

* Executive summaries
* Action items
* Open questions
* Risks and blockers
* Conflict tracking
* Speaker-based insights

---

# Skill Created

### Skill Name

brain-dump-action-planner

### Description

Transforms messy notes, meeting transcripts, brainstorming sessions, and stream-of-consciousness thoughts into structured summaries, action plans, decision logs, open questions, and task dashboards.
