# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## ABTalks 60 Days Claude Challenge

## Project Overview

Today I built **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab**, an interactive enterprise simulation that places the user in the role of a supply chain leader responsible for navigating a major operational disruption.

The simulator recreates real-world business decision making where every action impacts inventory, costs, supplier relationships, delivery performance, profitability, and customer satisfaction. Rather than focusing on theory, the application teaches practical trade-offs through an engaging simulation experience.

---

# Objective

Build a production-quality interactive Supply Chain Crisis Simulator using a single HTML file powered by React, CSS, and JavaScript that allows users to experience enterprise decision making under pressure.

---

# Features Implemented

## Welcome Experience

The simulator begins with an interactive introduction explaining:

- The company background
- Business objectives
- Crisis simulation workflow
- Learning outcomes
- Interactive decision process

A beginner-friendly onboarding ensures users understand each phase before making decisions.

---

# Random Company Generation

Each simulation generates a unique fictional enterprise including:

- Industry
- Annual Revenue
- Number of Factories
- Warehouses
- Active Suppliers
- Inventory Days
- Lead Time
- Sourcing Countries

Every playthrough creates a different operational profile, making the simulation highly replayable.

---

# Crisis Briefing

The simulator randomly generates realistic supply chain disruptions such as:

- Supplier Bankruptcy
- Factory Fire
- Port Strike
- Cyberattack
- Flood
- Political Conflict
- Raw Material Shortage
- Shipping Delay

Each crisis includes:

- Urgency Level
- Business Impact
- Operational Context
- Educational Explanation

---

# War Room Decision Making

Players enter the crisis war room and choose three response actions from six strategic options.

Example actions include:

- Expedite Air Freight
- Activate Backup Supplier
- Reallocate Inventory
- Customer Communication
- Renegotiate Contracts
- Emergency Automation

Each decision updates live business KPIs including:

- Cost
- Inventory
- Profit
- Delivery Speed
- Customer Satisfaction

The simulator emphasizes trade-offs rather than perfect solutions.

---

# Supplier Negotiation

A four-round negotiation module teaches supplier relationship management.

Negotiation metrics include:

- Trust
- Price Position
- Lead Time

Players must balance commercial terms while maintaining long-term partnerships.

The simulator demonstrates how negotiation outcomes affect overall resilience.

---

# CEO Boardroom

The executive boardroom challenges users with leadership-focused multiple-choice scenarios covering:

- Crisis Leadership
- Strategic Prioritization
- Operational Risk
- Stakeholder Communication
- Business Continuity

Leadership performance contributes to the overall executive score.

---

# AI Strategy Planning

Players invest in two AI-powered supply chain initiatives.

Available investments include:

- Demand Forecasting

- Supplier Risk Monitoring
- Warehouse Vision
- Procurement Copilot

The simulator estimates the operational impact of each investment across efficiency, resilience, and decision quality.
