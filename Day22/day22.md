# Day 22 – Validate Your Startup Idea Like a VC

## Objective

Today's challenge focused on using Claude as a Startup Advisor, VC Analyst, and Market Research Expert to transform a simple business idea into a structured startup validation report.

The goal was to evaluate market opportunity, customer pain points, founder-market fit, risks, competitors, and execution strategy before investing significant time and capital.

---

# Startup Overview

## Startup Idea

Affordable Dry Fruit Packaging Brand (India)

## Vision

To provide high-quality packaged dry fruits at affordable prices for lower and middle-income families by combining quality assurance, transparent sourcing, and value-focused packaging.

---

# Executive Summary

The startup targets a rapidly growing packaged dry fruits market while addressing a common consumer problem:

- High prices
- Poor quality consistency
- Adulteration concerns
- Lack of trusted affordable brands

Instead of competing on premium branding, the business focuses on:

- Better value
- Small affordable pack sizes
- Direct sourcing
- Trust and quality assurance
