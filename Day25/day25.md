# Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator

## Objective

Today's challenge focused on building a complete **AI Shark Tank Simulator** that allows founders to pitch their startup to AI investors and receive realistic investment feedback.

The simulator combines startup evaluation, investor psychology, scoring systems, business analysis, and an interactive user interface into a single HTML application.

---

# Project

## AI Shark Tank Simulator

This project simulates a startup pitch where four AI judges evaluate a business idea from different perspectives.

The application was developed as a single self-contained HTML file with no backend requirements.

---

# Features Implemented

## Startup Input

The simulator evaluates the following startup details:

* Startup Name
* Problem Statement
* Proposed Solution
* Revenue Model
* Target Audience
* Funding Ask

---

# AI Judges

Four independent AI judges participate in the pitch.

### 💼 Venture Capitalist

Focus

* Market Size
* Scalability
* Growth Potential

---

### 🚀 Founder

Focus

* Execution
* Team Capability
* Go-To-Market Strategy

---

### 🛒 Customer

Focus

* Customer Value
* Product Usefulness
* Pricing

---

### 💎 Angel Investor

Focus

* Profitability
* Unit Economics
* ROI
* Margins

---

# Startup Used

**TG Packaging Solution**

### Problem

Dry fruits become expensive because multiple middlemen increase the final selling price.

### Solution

A Direct-to-Consumer model sourcing dry fruits directly from farmers and delivering premium-quality products at affordable prices.

### Target Audience

Indian middle-class families.

### Revenue Model

Direct product sales.

### Funding Ask

₹1.5 Crore.

---

# Interactive Pitch Round

The simulator includes:

* Live conversation interface
* Judge introductions
* Two questions from each judge
* Founder responses
* Judge reactions
* Dynamic conversation flow

---

# Live Scorecard

The startup is evaluated on:

* Market Potential
* Innovation
* Business Model
* Execution
* Investment Worthiness

Scores are displayed using animated circular progress indicators.

---

# Investment Decision

At the end of the pitch the simulator generates:

* Investment Decision
* Suggested Valuation
* Funding Recommendation
* Investment Reasoning

Possible outcomes include:

* 🤝 Invest
* ❌ Reject
* 🏢 Acquire
* ⏳ Come Back Later
